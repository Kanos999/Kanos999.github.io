The Challenge

Those familiar with the popular Atari game from 1979 will know the fundamental machanics of Asteroids. The player controls a single spaceship in an asteroid field, avoiding collisions and shooting objects to earn points. This modern version implements a lot of those same mechanics, with the addition of multiplayer functionality. This introduces a whole new set of obstacles and things to shoot, and enhances the competitive nature of the game.

Technologies Used
